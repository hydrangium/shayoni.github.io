MY PROJECT

My project involves the detection of corona discharge in extremely high voltage transmission lines. My approach to achieving this goal is by using a Lucid Vision UV Camera to capture images of transmission lines where corona discharge is taking place.Then by using image processing and deep learning we tried to detect the exact location of corona discharge in the transmission lines.
